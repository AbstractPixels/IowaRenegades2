Art: More than just some pretty pictures

I’ve considered myself an artist for as long as I can remember, I don’t draw stick people anymore, my recent works consist of a mixture of digital abstract, typography and Arabic calligraphy. The underlying theme of my work is the capture of the feeling of continuous motion and change that a person experiences throughout this journey they call life. For those who aren’t fluent with artsy fartsy talk, I create art that has lots of 'whooshy' bits and vivid colours that looks great hung up on your wall.

Web: Code it, and they shall come

My fascination with website design began when I created a small online portfolio to exhibit my digital artwork and I've been hooked ever since.
I strive to create beautifully crafted, clean and innovative sites that are easy-to-use and feature intuitive navigation systems.
I use xHTML, copious amounts of CSS, a dash of javascript coding (jQuery is my bff) and a sprinkling of the grid-system.
I also work with web development companies to design gorgeous websites.
